Molten Passages are dangerous volcanic cave systems filled with lava, magma, and blackened stone formations.

## Overview

**Biome ID:** `MOLTEN_PASSAGES`  
**Vanilla Replacement:** `minecraft:dripstone_caves`  
**Color Code:** `#ED5D53`

## Terrain Palette

Molten Passages feature a dark, volcanic palette:

### Surface Layers (2 blocks deep)
- Stone
- Blackstone (primary)
- Black Concrete Powder
- Black Concrete

### Subsurface Layer (1 block deep)
- Stone
- Blackstone
- Black Concrete Powder
- Black Concrete

### Deepslate Transition
Below Y-level 7, terrain uses the `MOLTEN_PASSAGES_DEEPSLATE` palette.

<Note>
The dark palette creates dramatic contrast with the bright lava features, enhancing the volcanic atmosphere.
</Note>

## Features

### Preprocessors
- **Magma Streams** - Flowing magma formations
- **Lava Pools** - Large pools of lava

### Landforms
- **Charred Spikes** - Blackened stone spikes resembling volcanic formations

### Postprocessors
- **Cave Lava Columns** - Vertical lava flows

## Environment

Molten Passages are among the most hazardous cave biomes, with abundant lava creating constant danger. The charred spikes and blackstone terrain give these caves a volcanic, hellish appearance. These caves feel like natural transition zones to the Nether.

<Warning>
Exercise extreme caution when exploring Molten Passages due to the high concentration of lava features.
</Warning>
