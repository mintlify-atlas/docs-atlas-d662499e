## Generation Range

All Substratum cave biomes generate between Y-level **-63** and **300** by default, with deepslate replacing stone below Y-level **7**.

<Note>
The scale and distribution of cave biomes can be configured via `substratum_meta.yml`. The default cave biome scale is 200 blocks.
</Note>
