Amethyst Hollow biomes are spectacular crystalline caverns dominated by purple amethyst formations and budding crystals.

## Overview

**Biome ID:** `AMETHYST_HOLLOW`  
**Vanilla Replacement:** `minecraft:dripstone_caves`  
**Color Code:** `#BF85D6`

## Terrain Palette

Amethyst Hollow features a purple crystalline palette:

### Surface Layers (2 blocks deep)
- Stone
- Purple Concrete Powder
- Purple Concrete (dominant)
- Budding Amethyst
- Amethyst Block

### Deepslate Transition
Below Y-level 7, terrain uses the `AMETHYST_HOLLOW_DEEPSLATE` palette.

<Note>
The palette includes budding amethyst blocks naturally integrated into the terrain, making this biome valuable for renewable amethyst crystal farming.
</Note>

## Features

### Landforms
- **Ground Giant Amethyst Buds** - Large amethyst formations on cave floors
- **Ceiling Giant Amethyst Buds** - Crystal formations hanging from ceilings
- **Wall Giant Amethyst Buds** - Amethyst growths on cave walls

### Flora
- **Amethyst Buds** - Small decorative amethyst crystals

## Environment

Amethyst Hollow biomes are among the most visually striking cave biomes. The purple color scheme combined with crystal formations creates a magical, otherworldly atmosphere. Giant amethyst buds grow in all orientations, creating natural crystal gardens throughout the caves.

<Note>
These caves are excellent sources of amethyst shards and can provide renewable crystal farming opportunities due to naturally-placed budding amethyst blocks.
</Note>
