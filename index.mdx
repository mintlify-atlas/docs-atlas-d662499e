## Explore the Underground

Substratum transforms Minecraft's world generation into a completely underground experience, featuring 14 unique cave biomes with distinct themes, features, and terrain generation. From the glowing Honey Grotto to the icy Frozen Caverns, each biome offers a unique exploration experience.
